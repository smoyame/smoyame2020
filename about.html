My name is Samanta Moya Medina. I am a Graphic Design B.F.A candidate at the Maryland Institute College of Art and I also hold an Associate of Applied Science in Graphic Design from Montgomery College, Rockville.

My focus in Graphic Design lies primarily in print publications and web design. I maintain an interest in devoting a bulk of my skills to helping the psycology of the user parse the materials I design in an easy and friendly fashion.

My thesis project is currently on track to address disparities in paraeducation, with an emphasis on the virtual divide during COVID distance teaching.

My roots are in the experiences I had as a partial caregiver to my sisters who live with Down's Syndrome and RETT Syndrome, regular translator for my parents, and holder of responsibility for my three younger sisters. I volunteer here and there when I'm not helping my family.

Fun fact: I coded this website from scratch using HTML/CSS/Javascript. It might show here and there, but drop me a line and let me know about any major bugs you encounter! I was stuck using Chrome using Windows so I doubt this site is perfect. Hear from you soon!
