Hi, I'm Sam.

I am a Graphic Design B.F.A candidate at the Maryland Institute College of Art and I also hold an Associate of Applied Science in Graphic Design from Montgomery College, Rockville.

My focus in Graphic Design lies primarily in print publications and front-end web development. I maintain an interest in devoting a bulk of my skills to helping the psychology of the user parse the materials I design in an easy and friendly fashion.

My thesis project is on track to address disparities in paraeducation, with an emphasis on the virtual divide during COVID distance teaching.

My roots are in the experiences I had as a partial caregiver to my sisters who live with Down's Syndrome and RETT Syndrome, regular translator for my parents, and holder of responsibility for my three younger sisters. I volunteer here and there when I'm not helping my family.
