Hi, I'm Sam.

I am a Graphic Design B.F.A candidate at the Maryland Institute College of Art and I also hold an Associate of Applied Science in Graphic Design from Montgomery College, Rockville.

My focus in Graphic Design lies in typographic systems and front-end web development. I maintain an interest in using my skills to disseminate information for the greater good.

My thesis project is on track to address the social stigma against people with intellectual disabilities, with an emphasis on the exposed underfunding of programs during COVID.

My roots are in the experiences I had as a partial caregiver to my sisters who live with Down's Syndrome and RETT Syndrome, regular translator for my parents, and holder of responsibility for my three younger sisters.
