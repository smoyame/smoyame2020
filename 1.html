Publication

The Dry Run

I was challenged to create a newspaper based on the content of a story I hold dear to me. The Dry Run is an experimental broadsheet publication that is inspired by Daniel Keyes' Flowers for Algernon. This 3-page dry run explores Charlie's experiences in the form of complex typographical hierarchy.
